Examinations

A-level tests are held by examinations like Edexcel, OCR, AQA, WJEC, CCEA and CIE. Different boards have different timetable and subjects. Click the boards on the top to learn more about each examination.

Colleges Accepting A-level

There are a lot of countries accepting A-level tests:

  • Australia: All universities
  • Britain: All univeristies
  • Canada: University of Toronto, University of British Columbia, Queen's University, Western University, etc.
  • Hong Kong: All universities
  • Ireland: All universities
  • New Zealand: All universities
  • Singapore: All universities
  • South Africa: All universities

Education System

A-level is separated into two years:

  • First year: AS level, the students choose their favorite 3~4 courses and get AS Certificate after taking tests.
  • Second year: A2 level, the students choose best 3 courses from AS level, and get A-level Certificate after taking tests.

Register

If you are a school student, you should contact your school teacher to acquire information about how to register for A-level. If you are a self learner, check the official website of the board that you want to register for.
